Ramen Noodles

Description:

General Tso's Chicken is that perfect combination of sweet, savoury, spicy and tangy with crispy Chinese chicken bites.

Ingredients:

Sauce

  • Rice vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Corn starch

Chicken

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Put all of the sauce ingredients in a bowl and stir
  2. Cut chicken into 1-inch size cubes. Toss the chicken with corn starch.
  3. Cook your chicken until all sides are golden brown.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan.
  5. Saute garlic, ginger and pepper flakes in the pan.
  6. Add the sauce and bring to a simmer then add chicken.
  7. Toss the chicken, making sure to evenly coat each piece. Serve hot to hungry people!
